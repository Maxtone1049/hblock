Frequently Asked Questions

Explain the Difference in Plans to Me

Gold subscribers have their listings at the topmost of the search results; followed by listings on the Premium plan, which is then followed by those on Access plan. Listings on free plan come at the very bottom of search results.

What is Featured Listings?

Each plan have a finite number of listings that can be featured per month. This is like priority listing you want to market much faster. They are shown first before the rest of your other listings.

What is Boosted Listing?

These are special category of listing Ads that appear at the top of every search results; they are shown more often. At least one boosted listing appear for every search result. An algorithm ensures boosted listing have equal distribution.

What is Your Site Demography Like?

HUTBAY.com is visited by over 75,000 unique visitors on a monthly basis. Most visitors are from Nigeria, UK, USA, South Africa, Canada and other countries. The site is patronized by mostly professional home shoppers with a short conversion time.

Do You Help Post Listings?

Yes we do! For advertisers on any of our paying plans, we have dedicated customer support staff that attend to all your need on the portal. We help post your listings, manage it and update it at your request.

How Do I Get Started?

Simply choose a plan, select the number of months you'd like to subscribe for. Thereafter, you will be asked for your details (if not signed in already) and once payment is made, your subscription is activated and we can start helping you market your properties.
